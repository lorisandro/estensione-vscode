## TROUBLESHOOTING

### PROBLEMA: "No one is responding to my outreach"

**DIAGNOSI**:
- Message too generic?
- Target audience wrong?
- No social proof yet?

**FIX**:
- Hyper-personalize (mention specific thing from their profile)
- Offer free value first (quick audit, tip, small freebie)
- Add testimonials/portfolio to profile ASAP

### PROBLEMA: "I'm not closing clients"

**DIAGNOSI**:
- Calls going well but no commitment?
- Price objections?
- Timeline misalignment?

**FIX**:
- Ask: "What's holding you back from moving forward?"
- Address objections directly
- Offer smaller starting project (reduce risk)
- Show clear ROI in proposal

### PROBLEMA: "My product has no users"

**DIAGNOSI**:
- Poor launch execution?
- Product doesn't solve real pain?
- Bad positioning?

**FIX**:
- Re-launch (Product Hunt allows 1 re-launch)
- Talk to 10 potential users (understand their problems better)
- Improve landing page (clearer value prop)
- Paid ads ($50 budget to test)

### PROBLEMA: "I don't have enough time"

**DIAGNOSI**:
- Poor time management?
- Trying to do too much?
- Perfectionism?

**FIX**:
- Time block ruthlessly (3-hour deep work blocks)
- Cut 1 strategy (focus on freelance OR product first)
- Ship at 80% quality, iterate (done > perfect)
- Wake up 1 hour earlier
